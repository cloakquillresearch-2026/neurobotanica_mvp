## Introduction

NeuroBotanica ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of our users, research participants, and partners. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform for cannabinoid research, therapeutic optimization, and traditional knowledge validation.

This policy complies with applicable privacy laws including HIPAA, GDPR, CCPA, and state privacy regulations.

#### Research and Clinical Data
- Health information for therapeutic optimization

- **Direct Collection:** Information you provide when registering or using services

## Data Security

### Security Measures
- **Encryption:** AES-256 encryption for data at rest and in transit
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access with multi-factor authentication
- **Network Security:** Firewalls, intrusion detection, and monitoring
- **Physical Security:** Secure data centers with controlled access

### Data Retention
- **Clinical Data:** 7 years minimum (HIPAA requirement)
- **Research Data:** 10 years for validation and patent purposes
- **Account Data:** Retained during active use and 3 years after
- **Audit Logs:** 7 years for security and compliance

### Incident Response
- **Breach Notification:** Within 60 days for HIPAA breaches
- **Affected Individuals:** Direct notification of data exposure
- **Regulatory Reporting:** Required notifications to authorities
- **Remediation:** Immediate steps to contain and resolve incidents
